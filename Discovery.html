Discovery Phase

The Discovery phase of a company consists in the discovery and study of the target it addresses, thanks to community analysis and targeted surveys. With the collected data it is possible to create personas that emulate the typical behavior of a possible user. By tracking user journeys, it is possible to understand the site's pain points by revisiting the information hierarchy and the various pages. We can call it a redesign of the site, to make it efficient for its target. The discovery phase is essential for any company because it allows you to understand your weaknesses compared to your competitors in the market. Through various surveys aimed at the reference community, model users can be created on the basis of the data collected. These "personas", which emulate a typical customer, are useful for finding the pain points of the website by tracing their user journey, which is different from everyone. The result of the discovery phase is a redesign of the site, the creation of new sections and a new information hierarchy (IA), all based on your target.
